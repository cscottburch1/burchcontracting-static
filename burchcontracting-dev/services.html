Service FAQs

Common questions about decks, porches, garages, additions, and remodeling in Upstate SC.

What deck materials does Burch Contracting install?+

Pressure-treated lumber, hardwood, and composite materials such as Trex — chosen based on budget, maintenance preference, and how you plan to use the space.

How long does it take to build a custom deck?+

Most custom decks take 3 to 5 weeks from permit approval to completion, depending on size, complexity, and weather.

Can you convert my existing deck into a screened porch?+

Yes. Deck conversions are often 25–30% less expensive than new porch construction when the existing structure can support the roof and screen system.

Does Burch Contracting build detached and attached garages?+

Yes — two-car, three-car, and workshop configurations with options for electrical, insulation, and custom door placements.

Can you match my existing home when building an addition?+

Yes. Roofline, siding, windows, and trim are matched so the addition looks original to your home.

Does Burch Contracting do kitchen and bathroom remodels?+

Yes. Kitchen and bath renovations including tub-to-shower conversions, tile, cabinetry, flooring, and finish work.
