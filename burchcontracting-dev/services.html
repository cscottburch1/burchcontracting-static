Our Estimate Policy

We offer free consultations and ballpark estimates for every project — no cost, no obligation. Detailed written estimates, concept drawings, and floor plans take real time to prepare, so a fee applies for those — and it's fully credited toward your project if you hire us. This lets us keep ballpark pricing free for everyone exploring a project, while investing the time a serious, detailed estimate deserves for customers ready to move forward.

Common Questions

Each service page below has its own detailed FAQs. Here are the basics.

How do I get a free consultation and ballpark estimate?+

Call (864) 724-4600 or use the contact form to describe your project. Scott Burch will discuss your goals and provide a free ballpark price range. A detailed written estimate, concept drawings, or floor plans are available for a fee, fully credited back if you hire us.

What areas does Burch Contracting serve?+

Simpsonville, Fountain Inn, Mauldin, Greenville, Five Forks, Woodruff, Laurens, Gray Court, and surrounding Upstate SC communities.

Is Burch Contracting licensed and insured?+

Yes. Burch Contracting holds SC General Contractor License #CLG118679, is fully insured, and is BBB A+ rated.

Does Burch Contracting handle permits and inspections?+

Yes. We pull required building permits, coordinate HOA approvals when needed, and schedule inspections through local jurisdictions for every project we build.
