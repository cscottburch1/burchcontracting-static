About Burch Contracting

A South Carolina family business built on hard work, honest dealing, and a commitment to quality that shows in every project we touch.

Our Story

Built from the Ground Up

Burch Contracting was founded with a simple idea: build things right, treat people well, and stand behind the work. What started as a small crew taking on local jobs has grown into a full-service contracting operation serving communities across South Carolina.

Over the years, we've built relationships with the best subcontractors in the state, invested in skilled tradespeople, and earned a reputation for finishing what we start — on schedule and without surprises.

We're still locally owned. We still pick up the phone. And we still show up on the job site ourselves, because that's the only way to know that things are being done to the standard our clients deserve.
