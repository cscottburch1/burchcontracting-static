Common Questions

Straight answers from Scott Burch — licensed, local, and on your job site.

Is Burch Contracting a licensed general contractor in South Carolina?+

Yes. Burch Contracting holds SC General Contractor License #CLG118679. Scott Burch has been a licensed residential builder since 1995 and a licensed general contractor since 2014. The company is fully insured and BBB A+ rated.

What areas does Burch Contracting serve?+

Simpsonville, Fountain Inn, Mauldin, Greenville, Five Forks, Woodruff, Laurens, Gray Court, and surrounding Upstate SC communities. View all service areas.

How much does a deck cost in Upstate SC?+

Custom decks typically run $39 to $92 per square foot installed. Use the deck calculator for a planning estimate.

How much does a screened porch cost in Upstate SC?+

Screened porches typically run $15,000 to $65,000 depending on size and finishes. Deck conversions can save 50–70%. Use the porch calculator or request a free consultation.

How much does a detached garage cost?+

Detached two-car garages (576 sqft) commonly range from $58,000 to $70,000, with larger 3-car or workshop configurations (900 sqft) running $122,000 to $147,000. Use the garage calculator for a planning estimate.

How much does a room addition cost per square foot?+

Room additions typically cost $200 to $340 per square foot depending on scope and finishes. Use the addition calculator for a planning estimate.

Does Scott Burch work on the job site?+

Yes. You work directly with Scott — not a salesperson who disappears after you sign. Scott oversees quality, code compliance, and communication on every project.

Does Burch Contracting handle permits?+

Yes. We pull building permits, coordinate HOA approvals when needed, and schedule inspections in Greenville, Laurens, and Spartanburg counties.

How much does insurance restoration cost?+

Costs vary based on the extent of storm or water damage and the scope of repairs needed. We offer a free consultation and ballpark range. A detailed written estimate with full documentation is available for a fee, fully credited back if you hire us.

Does Burch Contracting handle ADA compliance projects?+

Yes. Burch Contracting builds residential accessibility modifications (roll-in showers, grab bars, ramps) and commercial ADA compliance upgrades (entrance ramps, restroom retrofits, door widening) to meet current ADA standards.
